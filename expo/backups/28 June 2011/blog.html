Takeover Code – Accessing sweetheart deals

Takeover regimes aim to deliver on two key promises – a better price than what may be availablein the stock market &/or a full exit which may not be available in the stock market. Mandatoryopen offers where a 100% exit is not available and the price is at or below prices available onthe stock exchange, are a waste of national resources. Worse, low priced open offers distort themarket and trap uninformed investors. Based on personal experience, I would welcome the draft regulations proposed by the Takeover Regulatory Advisory Committee (TRAC), as a significant step forward in the evolution of our capital markets... read more
