We are a small group of IIT Alumni and Teachers passionate about science.

We work with schools, parents and kids to make science fun and easy. We create experiments, models and activities to help open young minds to science in an engaging & exciting way.

We love to teach (and learn!)

We provide activity-based science program in association with schools

Our programs engage students and help them learn by:

  • Monitoring & evaluation them in realtime using our electronic response pads
