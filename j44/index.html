Clarity brings
vision

As an emblem of our belief in clarity and transparency, J44 Prabhat has no opaque walls.

Our intention was for the building to have no falseness, no facade. Just like your enterprise.

Daylight

Nothing but glass between you and natural light. The advantage – a naturally bright workday.

Visual Ambience

Clarity and transparency also let in the serene green environs of Prabhat Road directly into your workspace.

Full-scale windows

So that glass never becomes a wall. And also creates a greener building with lower AC consumption.

Smart Stairs

Goodbye, narrow stairways. Floating steel stairs suspended from three sides for floor access. Open is the new thing.

Structure Reveals
Strength

The strength that a structure provides is far greater than the sum of individual capabilities.

J44 prabhat completely does away with RCC and employs laser cut, pre-engineered steel members to bring it together.

leaner, cleaner, stronger, smarter. bankable.

Cutting Edge

Ultra-modern pre-fab techniques allow us to pin-join the steel members, not weld them. The result is a stronger building.

metal deck

Metal decks form the slabs of J44 Prabhat, not RCC. Advantages – increased ceiling height, efficient flooring options and cable trunking.

Element Resistance

The steel underpinnings of the building make J44 Prabhat more fire-resistant than an equivalent RCC structure. Earthquake resistance is also enhanced.

Construction Speed

Pre-fabrication allows for minimal construction time, since it is only assembly that takes place on site. Quicker possesions for you.

Efficiency Expands Possibilities

To achieve more with less and create magic on the way – that has been the way for the Jalan Group.

J44 Prabhat reflects this same approach. be it materials or systems, everything is geared towards being more efficient.

Efficiency and its value. we know that you know.

Less is More

Narrower walls and smaller columns liberate almost 100 sq.ft of carpet area on every floor. More space for you to explore.

Work Smart

J44 Prabhat’s ultra-modern building management system ensures perfectly filtered access for your clients and employees.

Cool Conscious

The building’s VRV air-conditioning system all the ACs in the building from a common compressor, yet allows for completely independent cooling control.
